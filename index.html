EBR Energy was established in 2009 and is a top Leading Company in Solar energy solutions in Pakistan. EBR Energy is an energy integrator and a renewable energy solutions provider with specialized expertise in solar technologies and solar solutions. EBR Energy is one of the best solar power companies in Pakistan.

Our name stands for experience, quality, and competence and is reflected in the work we do. At EBR Energy, we provide complete net metering solutions, engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC), including post-commissioning, services for a variety of renewable energy requirements, regardless of size and location. We have extensive experience in turn-key projects for various customer segments, and our global presence and experience ensure that our customers will receive the best technology at the most competitive prices.
